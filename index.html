Recommendations

Suggested actions ranked by continuity and operational safety.

Automate low-risk administrative duties

Move inbox sorting, interview scheduling, reporting assembly, and template document prep into workflow tools first.

Convert tribal knowledge into SOPs

High-risk operational roles should be documented before any reassignment or staffing change is considered.

Reassign, don't abruptly remove

Build coverage maps so critical duties can shift gradually to managers, shared-service teams, or software with fallback ownership.
